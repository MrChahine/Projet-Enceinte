Project Details

Completed
Key Tasks
Component Selection

Identified and selected high-quality components including the Dayton Audio PC83-8 drivers, the WONDOM JAB2-30 V2 amplifier, the WONDOM ICP5 DSP programmer, and the 18650 battery charger. Choices were based on power efficiency, audio performance, and integration ease.

Electronics Assembly & Wiring

Assembled and wired all internal components: amplifier, Bluetooth module, speaker drivers, battery system (3×18650), and the WONDOM AA-JA11113 charger. Ensured clean routing, secure soldering, and safe power distribution.

DSP Configuration & Audio Profiling

Programmed the audio DSP of the JAB2 module using the WONDOM ICP5 interface. Defined and tested several filter profiles (e.g., bass boost, flat EQ, vocal clarity) to adapt sound output to different environments.

Support in Enclosure CAD Modeling

Advised teammates during the 3D modeling phase on SolidWorks. Helped optimize internal volume, port placement, and acoustic isolation zones based on component layout and acoustic requirements.
